## 📑 Quick Reference

### Configuration
- **File**: `src/config/dashboard.ts`
- **Contains**: Sheet ID, date range, weights, thresholds, colors
- **Documentation**: See QUICK_START.md "Customization" section

### Google Sheets Setup
- **Format**: DD-MM-YYYY (sheet tab names)
- **Columns**: 10 required columns in specific order
- **Permissions**: "Anyone with the link can view"
- **Documentation**: See README.md "Data Structure" section

### Filters & Controls
- **Date Filters**: Specific date, month, date range
- **Member Filters**: Individual or all members
- **Sort Options**: Score, connections, leads, conversion
- **Documentation**: See FEATURES.md "Date & Time Filtering System"
